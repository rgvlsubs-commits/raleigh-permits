Understanding the Data ▼

What's included

  • New business buildings only - brand new construction where businesses will operate.
  • Office - Banks, professional buildings, corporate offices
  • Retail - Stores, shopping centers, mercantile buildings
  • Industrial - Warehouses, manufacturing, distribution centers
  • Entertainment - Recreation facilities, amusement venues
  • Hospitality - Hotels, motels, tourist accommodations
  • Healthcare - Medical offices, clinics, hospitals
  • Institutional - Churches, schools, public facilities

What's excluded

  • Residential - Apartments, condos, and housing (see Housing page)
  • Renovations - Alterations to existing buildings
  • Infrastructure - Parking garages, sheds, fences

Data from City of Raleigh Building Permits. Shows new construction from 2020 onwards.
